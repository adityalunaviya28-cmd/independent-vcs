The Problem We're Solving
Thousands of aspiring VCs are stuck at the gate — no MBA, no PE background, no metro address. We believe the best deal-finders aren't always hired. So we're building our own table.
The Gap
VC hiring is gated
Most VC roles go to MBA grads, ex-PE/consulting professionals in metro cities. Talent from tier-2 cities with non-traditional backgrounds rarely gets a shot — not because they lack ability, but because they lack access.
Our Answer
Stop waiting. Start doing.
Instead of competing for a seat, we build our own practice. Each member sources real deals, builds real theses, and talks to real founders — the exact work a VC analyst does on day one.
Who This Is For
Early-career, non-traditional
No MBA. No IIT. No Bengaluru address. Just genuine curiosity about startups, hunger to learn, and willingness to roll up your sleeves and build something from scratch — together.
The Real Goal
Network is deal-flow
In VC, networking is everything. Every conversation with a founder, every thesis document, every memo co-built here becomes a living portfolio — and a pipeline. Deal-flow is the by-product of genuine relationships.
